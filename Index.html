Your Mission

On a war-torn island, private military contractors fight for control of valuable gear and scarce resources. From the sunlit streets of the suburbs to the shadowy depths of smuggling tunnels, every encounter will push your strategy and instincts to their limits.

Contractors: ExfilZone combines realistic combat mechanics, tense decision making, and immersive environments to deliver a high stakes tactical experience like no other.

Dynamic Extraction

In-Depth Weapon Customization

Realistic Gunplay

Tactical Teamplay
